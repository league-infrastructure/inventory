# Checkout history should retain all entries and appear at bottom

## Summary

On the Kit detail page, the checkout history section should:

1. Move to the bottom of the page (after packs and computers).
2. Display all checkout/check-in history entries, not just the most
   recent one. Currently, checking in and out multiple times only shows
   one entry in the history list.
